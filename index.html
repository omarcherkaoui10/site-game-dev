Description

GameDev Club is a dynamic community dedicated to the exciting world of game development, with a particular focus on Pygame and Godot technologies. Comprised of passionate students from diverse academic backgrounds, we come together to explore the art and science of game creation. Our mission is to foster creativity, collaboration, and learning through hands-on experiences. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced developer, our club provides a welcoming space to enhance your skills, participate in projects, and connect with like-minded individuals who share your interests.

   

Formation

PYGAME
Pygame is a popular and versatile Python library designed for creating 2D games and multimedia applications. It provides a straightforward way for developers to work with graphics, sound, and user input, making it an excellent choice for beginners and experienced programmers alike.

GODOT
Godot is a popular open-source game engine that has gained a strong following in the game development community. Known for its user-friendly interface, robust feature set, and the flexibility it offers to developers, Godot has become a go-to choice for both beginners and experienced game developers
